Mother Mary Teresa Bojaxhiu

1910 - 1997

Teresa founded the Missionaries of Charity, a Roman Catholic religious congregation that had over 4,500 nuns and was active in 133 countries

During her lifetime Mother Teresa became famous as the Catholic nun who dedicated her life to caring for the destitute and dying in the slums of Calcutta - now known as Kolkata.
The most well-known portrait of Teresa.

The following is a list of few things about Mother Teresa

  • 1910 - She was born in Skopje (now the capital of North Macedonia).
  • 1928 - After living in Skopje for eighteen years, she moved to Ireland and then to India, where she lived for most of her life.
  • 1950 - Teresa founded the Missionaries of Charity, a Roman Catholic religious congregation that had over 4,500 nuns
  • 1962 - Teresa received a number of honors, including the Ramon Magsaysay Peace Prize
  • 1979 - She received a nobel peace prize.
  • 2016 - She was canonised.
